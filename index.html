Why Annie?

Typing

  • Universal design based on the ubiquitous Brailler.
  • Soft keys that are much easier to press than those of a mechanical typewriter

Self Learning

  • Annie lets your child play and learn Braille all by themselves.
  • Assign homework and schedule tests for your child to complete at their own pace.

Reading

  • Two large cells introduce the child to Braille alphabets and numbers.
  • 6 standard-sized Braille cells cover all primary learning needs including contractions

Audio guided

  • Annie talks to the child and guides them through the Braille lessons, games and tests.
  • Learn by yourself using your own earphones or with your parents and teachers using the inbuilt speakers

Child-friendly

  • Super Light-weight. Weighs less than 1kg.
  • Only child-friendly, parental approved educational content.

Data tracking

  • Monitor usage of the child.
  • Track learning progress and outcomes.
