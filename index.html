Here's why Annie is the right Braille-learning device for your child

Typing

Universal design based on the ubiquitous Perkins Brailler. Soft keys that are easy to press.

Writing

A one-of-its-kind digital slate that mimics writing on paper. Softer to press comparerd to paper

Reading

Two large cells introduce the child to Braille. 6 standard-size Braille cells work in conjunction with the typing and writing modules for simultaneous learning.

Audio feedback

Annie talks to the child and guides them theough the lessons. Inbuilt speakers and earphone jack provide multiple sources of sound.

Child-friendly

Super Light-weight. Weighs 400 grams. Only child-friendly educational content.

Data tracking

Monitor usage of the child. Track learning progress and outcomes.
